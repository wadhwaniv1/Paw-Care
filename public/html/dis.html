You Have A Common Cold

Symptoms of a common cold include:

  • Runny Nose
  • Congestion
  • Coughing
  • Sneezing
  • Sore Throat

You may also experience:

  • mild headache
  • mild body aches

You Have A Flu

Symptoms of a flu include:

  • Runny Nose
  • Congestion
  • Coughing
  • Sneezing
  • Sore Throat
  • Fatigue
  • Body Aches
  • Headache
  • Nausea

You may also experience:

  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
